# Gaps

- **Fixed foresight basis.** DINOv2 + DPAv3 is hand-picked; no per-task or per-timestep selection over geometric / semantic / motion / tactile / affordance targets. A *mixture-of-VFM-teachers* with learned or RL-gated weights is untested.
- **Self-teacher quality ceiling.** Student cannot exceed the VAM's own multi-step generation — if VDM hallucinates or is mis-calibrated, the "teacher" silently misleads. No GT-anchored correction term, no teacher uncertainty estimate, no ensemble-of-teachers baseline.
- **Why video VFMs lose to image VFMs is unexplained.** V-JEPA2 / VideoMAEv2 underperform DINOv2 — if true in general, it undercuts the "temporal foresight" premise. Needs controlled probing (are video VFMs low-res? low-channel-count? misaligned in temporal stride?) and a recipe to make video VFMs useful targets.
- **Trajectory-alignment claim is under-tested.** The ablation swaps self-generated V̂ for GT future and drops 4.16→3.82, but confounds several effects (domain gap, VDM error copying). Needed: controlled study with (i) stronger VDM, (ii) noise-matched GT features, (iii) mixed GT+self teacher.
- **Single-step shortcut is a one-trick cut.** No combination with consistency-distillation, flow-matching one-step VDMs, or LCM-style backbones, which could collapse the 231 ms VDM cost dominating the 307 ms budget.
- **Action chunk = 8, closed-loop only at 25 Hz.** Reactivity inside chunks vs. foresight staleness is not measured; replan-frequency sweep and disturbance-rejection tests are missing.
- **No out-of-distribution / embodiment-transfer study.** All real tasks share one Cobot and one front camera; no new-embodiment, wrist-cam, depth-cam, or deformable / contact-rich task evaluation. Robustness of the distilled foresight when the VDM's priors are stressed is unknown.
- **Uni-Perceiver carries a large share of the gain.** Removing it drops 4.16→3.72 (larger than removing any single decoupler). Suggests the learnable-query condensation, not the distillation itself, may be the dominant lever — needs orthogonal ablation (plug Uni-Perceiver onto VPP features directly).
- **No analysis of what the decouplers actually denoise.** PCA visualizations only; no quantitative measure of (a) temporal coherence gain, (b) residual leakage from one-step features after decoupling, (c) whether the "reference anchor" concat can be removed without collapse.
- **No scaling law / data-efficiency plot.** Claim of data efficiency vs direct VLA is asserted but not quantified — e.g., performance vs # demonstrations curve against Spatial Forcing / π0.
- **Three-stage frozen pipeline precludes end-to-end co-adaptation.** Whether joint fine-tuning (expert signal back to decouplers / VDM) helps or hurts is untested, though it would be a natural next step for the paradigm.
- **Safety / failure modes of hallucinated foresight.** If the decoupler outputs a confidently wrong future (e.g., DPAv3-style depth for an occluded object), the action expert will commit to it. No abstention, confidence head, or fall-back mechanism is proposed.
