# Gaps

- **"Planning" is single-shot, not search.** CLaD predicts one latent foresight per step and FiLM-conditions the diffusion policy. Nothing rolls out alternative z_dyn candidates, re-scores them, or selects among them — so the cross-modal dynamics is never used as a simulator. Opportunity: MPC / beam search / CEM over latent foresights at inference, using L_recon-decoded consistency or a learned value head as the score.
- **No recursive / long-horizon latent rollout.** Foresight horizon τ=6 is fixed and never chained. Unclear whether z_dyn composes across multiple τ-steps or drifts. Opportunity: study multi-step rollout stability and train with curriculum over τ.
- **Foresight never receives task reward.** Stage-1 is pure SSL (EMA + recon); Stage-2 policy is frozen on foresights. Foresight errors are not corrected by downstream success. Opportunity: reward-conditioned or policy-gradient-aligned foresight fine-tuning; foresight-as-critic.
- **Asymmetry is task-specific.** Proprio-queries-semantic wins on LIBERO tabletop manipulation by <1 pt over the reverse direction. Unclear for mobile manipulation, bimanual, tactile/force, or humanoid. Opportunity: learned or gated asymmetry conditioned on embodiment/task.
- **Compact latent loses fine spatial detail.** Task 9 perceptual ambiguity drops SR to 81.3%. Authors point at object-centric latents — but no experiment. Opportunity: slot-attention or 3D-token foresights, or foresights with explicit object slots.
- **Stage-1 not amortized across data.** 22 h per task-family on a single 4090 with only LIBERO-LONG data. Opportunity: pretrain z_dyn on OXE/DROID, test zero-shot transfer of cross-modal dynamics to unseen embodiments.
- **Only sim, only LIBERO.** No real-robot, no distractors, no lighting/domain shift, no LIBERO-Spatial/Object/Goal result for CLaD itself reported inline (Table 6 shows baselines only). Opportunity: report CLaD on all LIBERO suites + real-robot.
- **No comparison to search-based latent planners.** LBP (backward search), UVA, and Dreamer-style MPC operate in latent space but are compared only on end-task SR, not on their search behaviour vs CLaD's foresight. Opportunity: unified benchmark of latent planners under matched compute.
- **Action-token masking is under-explored.** Only used as a training trick; not leveraged as an inverse-dynamics head at inference. Opportunity: use the masked-action path to *infer* actions given predicted foresights, giving a second planning route.
- **Evaluation protocol inconsistency.** Baselines reported over mixed 20-/50-rollout protocols and different source papers. Weakens head-to-head claims; re-run under one protocol.
